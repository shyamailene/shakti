Sudha Purihella

General Secretary

Sudha Purihella, is a native of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. Currently a US citizen from 1988 and lives in Northern Virginia, USA with her family. She takes pride in being a professional, mom and wife and believes in juggling the two professional and personal worlds to maintain a health work life balance, which aided in her philosophy of raising two healthy children and still maintained her professional status since 1989. She holds a B.Sc., in Home Science from Hyderabad, India, and two master’s degrees, MA in Psychology and MA in Mental Health Counseling and has worked in the field of Psychology and Mental Health since 1989. In her bachelor’s program in Hyderabad 1984-86, as part of her curriculum she participated in several Indira Gandhi Community Empowerment projects in rural areas near Hyderabad, like Building smokeless Chulas (stoves), educated rural population on importance of family planning, taught skills in the importance of personal hygiene, assertive communication and mental health awareness.

In Washington, D.C. since 1989, She worked in Community Based Mental Health-Psychiatric Residential Rehabilitation programs for adults for 20 years and has left as a Senior Director of Clinical Services in 2005 to start her journey in out-patient psychotherapy private practice in downtown Washington DC called Sahaya Therapy, LLC. She has successfully completed 12 years in her own private practice. Her practice believes in cultural and gender equality, mutual respect, psycho-education, improve and increase knowledge of self, self-preservation and stress management will increase awareness of self. She believes exercise, yoga and meditation will assist in attaining these goals of higher functioning.

Her passion in Psychology, Behavioral Sciences and Community Based Programs in Behavioral and Mental Health Awareness, belief in Gender equality and humanitarian service to people led her to join Shakti-US a non- profit organization for women’s empowerment in 2017. She values relationships and believes that all individuals are existentially unique and need to be treated with dignity, value and respect.

Dr.Kavya K. Manyapu

Advisory Member

Dr.Kavya K. Manyapu is a Space Scientist currently working in Flight Crew Operations and Testing at The Boeing Company building NASA’s next Commercial Human Spacecraft called Starliner CST-100. She also supports the International Space Station Program Flight operations at the NASA Johnson Space Center as a Boeing MER Duty Officer.

She holds a B.S in Aerospace Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology, Masters from MIT in Aeronautics and Astronautics and a Ph.D. in Aerospace Sciences from the University of North Dakota where she has worked on patent pending next generation spacesuit technologies. She has presented technical publications at multiple national and international conferences based on her research. She worked on several space projects with various companies including Lockheed Martin, Georgia Tech, NASA, MIT and others. She served as a crew member for analog Space/Mars Missions at NASA and Mars Society simulating, training and developing technologies for Human Mars and long duration missions.

She is a private pilot, certified advanced underwater SCUBA diver, aerobatic pilot, an avid hiker and a Bharatanatyam dancer. She currently serves as a Regional Officer for a United Nations spun International organization called Space Generation Advisory Council and holds leadership positions in other aerospace related organizations. Apart from her day job, Kavya enjoys mentoring, educating and inspiring students to pursue their passions and careers in STEM fields and has reached out at local, national and international levels. She was featured in a few documentaries on space exploration. She is passionate about human space exploration and the future it holds for the betterment of mankind.
